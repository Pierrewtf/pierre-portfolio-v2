About The Artist

Creativity shaped by culture, emotion, and storytelling.

Agu Chiagoziem Pierre is a multidisciplinary visual artist and graphic designer passionate about storytelling through visual expression.

Inspired by comics, street-art influences, contemporary African aesthetics, and digital experimentation, his work blends bold creativity with emotional depth and strong visual identity.

A graduate of Afe Babalola University, Ekiti (2022), Pierre explores painting, mural art, digital illustration, sculpting, branding, and visual design.
